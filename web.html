Why Friends of the Trees

The National Society of the Friends of the Trees (NSFOT) has been India's premier environmental education and conservation organization for over six decades.

Since our founding in 1957, we've dedicated ourselves to fostering a deep appreciation for trees, plants, and the natural world through engaging exhibitions, educational workshops, and meaningful community initiatives that inspire environmental stewardship across generations.

  • 60+ years of environmental legacy and dedication
  • Annual exhibitions attracting thousands of visitors
  • Educational workshops on sustainable horticulture
  • Community-driven conservation initiatives
  • Promoting organic and sustainable gardening
